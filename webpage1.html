WHAT IS MENTAL HEALTH?

Mental health includes a person's condition regarding their emotional, social, and psychological well being. It's all about how we think, feel, and act. Your mental health can change over time.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

It can help you deal with stressful problems, become productive and successful, and have good relationships with other people. Mental health can affect your physical health as well.

WHAT MAY AFFECT MY MENTAL HEALTH?

Things that may affect your mental health are: family history, life experiences (especially traumatic events), brain chemistry, and your lifestyle such as physical activity.

MENTAL DISORDERS

Mental disorders are serious and common conditions that affects how people function every day. These can be treated, and if you think you have a mental health problem, don't be afraid to ask for help.
