Mental Health Awareness Among Filipino Youth

According to a survey by the World Health Organization held in 2015, ten to fifteen percent of Filipino children are affected by mental health problems. You may find a list of mental health programs in the Philippines here.

For PSHS-MC Scholars

Organizations

Kandili is PSHS-MC's official student mental health committee. They aim to spread mental health awareness within the Pisay community. Unfortunately, not many people are aware that this organization exists. They hold wellness programs and share self-care tips. You may find them on Facebook. There is also an organization called Solace, which is PSHS-MC's women empowerment and sexual harrassment intervention committee. You may find them on Facebook and Twitter.

GCU

PSHS's guidance counseling unit is a safe space for Pisay scholars, for both mental health and educational concerns. They hold Care Month, to promote mental health awareness in October since World Mental Health Day is on October 10. You may find them on Facebook.
